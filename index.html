What is a Sprite?

Sprites, are two dimensional bitmap images that form part of a larger scene. They can be combined in multiple roles to create an animation. These are usually seen in video games and animations.


A combination of sprites is called a Sprite-Sheet. This is a collection of small sprites which allows one to create a flow of animation through a specific sequence of sprites. However, these sprite sheets have their own pros and cons.

  • Pros :
    1. They have a small digital footprint
    2. Fast
    3. Efficient
    4. Predictable
  • Cons :
    1. They are not always fluid
    2. Limited
